Our Favourite Recipes

Chocolate Cake

Indulge in our rich and moist chocolate cake recipe

Ingredients:

  • 200g cake flour
  • 200g suger
  • 75g cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 200ml milk
  • 100ml vegetable oil
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions:

1. Preheat oven to 180 degrees celcius.

2. Mix dry ingredients in one bowl and wet ingredients in another.

3. Combine both mixtures and pour into a greased cake pan.

4. Bake for 30-35 minutes.

Classic Croissant

Try our easy and delightfull croissant recipe.

Ingredients:

  • 500g all-purppose flour
  • 300ml water
  • 10g salt
  • 40g suger
  • 25g yeast
  • 250g unsalted butter

Instructions:

1. Mmix flour,water,salt,sugar, and yeast to form dough.

2. Knead and let it rise for 1 hour.

3. Roll out the dough and incorporate butter.

4. Fold and roll sevetral times,then shape into croissants.

5. Bake at 200 degrees celcius.
